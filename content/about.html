Professional

During my 15 years delivering software in the finance industry I have worked with accountants, bookkeepers, investment consultants, human resources and compliance to deliver tools primarily to boost efficiency but also to help keep in-line with the ever changing requirements surrounding data law. I have worked on...

  • Accounting systems (accounts, trial balances, etc.)
  • Book keeping systems (reconciling, bank statement import, etc.)
  • Automated billing systems
  • Engines to search databases against UN, UK and US sanction list entries for compliance purposes
  • Bespoke charting of large datasets
  • Bespoke report generation with lots of rules surrounding what pages, charts, text, etc. are displayed
  • Investment portfolio comparison
  • Interfaces to old systems such as VMS and Delphi to alleviate the burden of changing to new systems
  • Content Management Systems and Customer Relationship Management
  • Building software against existing legacy databases to deprecate and replace old software

Beyond the finance industry I also create websites for small local companies, self-employed people and charities. I'm not sure why these have been smaller operations, perhaps because I have a soft spot for people trying to start something new. As I host a lot of personal projects I have quite a few virtual servers hosted in the UK and occasionally provide managed hosting.
