In addition to the projects listed below, I've explored various other interests, including creating wallpapers with Blender, developing Firefox themes, and even publishing a spreadsheet to assist people with navigating Guernsey's complex voting system.

data.gg

data.gg is the brainchild of Jason Magee and Kieran Senior, aimed at providing all Bailiwick of Guernsey-related data to the public through an easily accessible API available in multiple formats. We currently support XML, JSON, and HTML, with plans to include Object data formats and gzipped formats in the future. data.gg is and will always remain a non-profit initiative.
